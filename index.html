About LIBA

Loyola Institute of Business Administration (LIBA) is a premier B school, established in 1979 within the premises of Loyola College, to form competent and committed leaders who are ethical, principle centered and socially responsible with a global perspective and entrepreneurial spirit.

LIBA is managed by Jesuits of the Loyola College Society, well known for their outstanding contribution to higher education.

LIBA stands for excellence with ethics which are the hallmarks of Jesuit business education, and all its programs and activities embody these two elements, and it also earnestly strives to inculcate in the students the values of excellence, justice, honesty, and service to society.


About LIBAzaar

LIBAzaar is the annual mega carnival of LIBA which is all set to provide greater holiday experience to visitors, by offering interesting competitions, games and performances alongside street shopping and dining experiences, through a wide range of stalls ranging from branded and street food outlets to apparels and handicrafts.

LIBAzaar also has fun cultural events such as Music, Fashion Show, Dance, Music-Solo Singing, Adapt tune, Coloring, Drawing, Spell bee, Craft Competition, Shipwreck, Channel Surfing, Block and Tackle, Video Creation Competition.

Amazing Gift Vouchers and Certificates will be given to the winners. Join us in creating cool memories this 2nd February 2025.
