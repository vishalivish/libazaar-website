Spell Bee (Age: 8-10)

Rules:

  • The pronouncer announces the word to be spelt.

  • Each participant will be given 2 words.

  • Participants have 15 seconds to begin spelling the word after it is announced.

  • The speller listens carefully to the pronouncer and asks for the word to be repeated if necessary.

  • When the speller is sure he/she understands the word, pronounces it, spells it, and then says the word again. He/she must say it loudly enough for the judge to hear it.

  • The judge(s) determines whether the word was spelt correctly

  • Points will be awarded for each correctly spelled word.

  • The speller who spells the word in a least timing will be awarded the points.

  • Prizes will be awarded to the top three winners with maximum points.
